Preference

Icons follow a rounded, filled style with consistent weights. Emoji-style icons for delight moments; line icons for navigation and functional UI. All icons must meet WCAG 2.1 AA contrast.

08 — Principles

Design Principles

01
Accessible First
Every feature should feel achievable. Clear language, high contrast, legible type at every size.
02
Reduce Effort
Remove friction wherever possible. One tap to save a recipe. Two taps to build a list.
03
Real-Life Solutions
Design for busy, tired, real people — not idealized meal planners with unlimited time.
04
Never Judge
No guilt. No "you should." Support every user's choices and make decisions feel right.
