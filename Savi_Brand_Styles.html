04Mark Anatomy

Every element has a purpose

The mark is composed of intentional elements that together tell the Savi story at a glance.

Leaf Accent on the “i”

The forest-green leaf crowns the “i” dot, signalling freshness and real, wholesome food — a visual shorthand for the natural quality Savi champions.

Golden Wordmark

Warm gold (#F0C84A) on dark backgrounds conveys value and reward without cliché imagery. On light surfaces, the wordmark shifts to deep navy for maximum legibility.

Bold Rounded Letterforms

The heavy, rounded strokes feel approachable and trustworthy — a confident partner, never cold or corporate.

Tagline: Two Beats

“Eat well. Spend wisely.” — two short, punchy sentences. The period after each creates a decisive, memorable rhythm at any size.

07Usage Rules

Protect the mark

Consistent, disciplined use of the logo builds brand recognition. These rules keep the mark clear, legible, and on-brand.

✓ Do

Use the reversed wordmark (yellow #F0C84A) on dark backgrounds: Savi Navy, Deep Forest, or Mid Forest. These are the primary dark surface pairings.

✗ Don’t

Don’t place the yellow wordmark on light surfaces (cream, white, yellow). It does not meet WCAG AA contrast and loses legibility at small sizes.

✓ Do

Use the standard navy wordmark (#1E2E3D) on light surfaces: Warm Cream, White, or Brand Yellow. The dark letterforms achieve 14.5:1 contrast on white.

✗ Don’t

Don’t use the navy wordmark on dark backgrounds. Use only the reversed (yellow) version on any surface darker than 50% grey.

✓ Do

Scale the logo proportionally. Use the SVG file at any size — the vector artwork remains crisp from favicon to billboard without rasterisation artefacts.

✗ Don’t

Don’t stretch, compress, rotate, or distort the wordmark. Never alter the relative size or position of the leaf accent on the “i”.

✓ Do

Maintain a clear space equal to the cap-height of the “S” on all sides. This keeps the mark readable and free from visual clutter in any layout.

✗ Don’t

Don’t recolor the leaf, add drop shadows, outlines, glows, or gradients to the wordmark. The green leaf (#2F9B72) is a non-negotiable brand constant.

08Design Rationale

Why Savi looks the way it does

Every design decision serves the mission: helping people eat well and spend wisely.

🌿

The Leaf

Crowning the “i” with a green leaf grounds Savi in real, fresh, natural food — signalling quality ingredients, not processed shortcuts.

✨

Gold = Value

Warm gold telegraphs “savings done right” — quality, reward, and warmth — without resorting to cliché dollar signs or discount aesthetics.

🤝

Two Wordmark Versions

A reversed (gold) and a standard (navy) version ensure the mark is always legible and on-brand, whether printed on packaging, displayed in-app, or used on signage.

📱

Digitally Versatile

SVG vector files scale from 20 px favicon to 4 K billboard without quality loss — exactly what a modern, multi-platform food-tech brand requires.
