06 — Brand Color Tokens

The palette in this logo

Gold tones are used exclusively in the icon mark and on dark backgrounds — they fail WCAG AA on white. On light/white backgrounds, "Saves" uses Deep Forest (#1A3D2B), which achieves 10.9:1 contrast. Usage tags below show each token's appropriate context.

07 — Usage Rules

Protect the mark

Consistent, disciplined use of the logo builds brand recognition. Follow these rules to keep the mark clear, legible, and on-brand.

✓ Do
Use the icon mark on a forest green or cream background for maximum contrast and brand recognition.
✕ Don't
Don't place the mark on a background that clashes with either the gold heart or the green leaf — e.g., orange or bright red.
✓ Do
Always pair "Food" in Plus Jakarta Sans 800 with "Saves" in DM Serif Display Italic. The contrast between the two typefaces is intentional.
✕ Don't
Don't stretch, compress, or distort the heart mark. Scale proportionally using the icon's square bounding box.
✓ Do
Use the monochrome (white or forest) version when printing on single-color media, embossing, or watermarking.
✕ Don't
Don't recolor the leaf to any color other than the green family (#2F9B72 – #1A3D2B). The green leaf is a brand constant.
✓ Do
Maintain a minimum clear space equal to the height of the "F" in "Food" around all sides of the full lockup.
✕ Don't
Don't add drop shadows, outlines, glows, or decorative effects to the mark. Let its gradients and clean geometry carry the visual weight.
08 — Design Rationale

Why a heart and a leaf?

🤍
Care & Trust
The heart communicates that FoodSaves genuinely cares about helping families stretch their budgets — it's not just a utility app, it's a partner.
🌿
Fresh & Natural
The green leaf at the crown grounds the mark in real, fresh food — not processed or packaged goods — reinforcing the brand's connection to quality ingredients.
✨
Gold = Value
Warm gold is universally associated with value, quality, and reward. The gold heart visually telegraphs "savings" — a golden opportunity — without resorting to cliché dollar signs.
📱
Digitally Versatile
The bold silhouette reads clearly at 20px as a favicon, at 1024px on a billboard, and everywhere in between — exactly what a modern app icon requires.
